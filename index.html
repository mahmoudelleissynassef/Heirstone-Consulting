A Four-Step Engagement Model

No off-the-shelf frameworks. Every mandate is shaped to context, objectives and constraints — and led from the front by senior partners.

01

Understand

Intensive diagnostic. We immerse in the client's context, market environment and strategic objectives — and challenge the brief where it needs challenging.

02

Analyse

Rigorous quantitative and qualitative analysis — market research, financial modelling, benchmarking and stakeholder mapping — to generate evidence-based insight.

03

Strategise

Analysis becomes strategy. Phased recommendations, prioritised initiatives and a clear-eyed view of risk, sequencing and capital implications.

04

Deliver

Outputs designed for decision-makers — concise, board-ready and built to be presented at sovereign, ministerial and investment-committee level.

About Heirstone Consulting & Strategic Advisory

Heirstone is a specialist strategy advisory for frontier and high-growth markets — the environments where data is limited, decisions are high-stakes, and conventional consulting frameworks break down.

With offices in Dubai and Cairo, the firm pairs Tier-1 advisory pedigree with deep regional knowledge. We work at the intersection of business ambition and institutional reality, and our remit spans single-asset positioning to national transformation programmes.
