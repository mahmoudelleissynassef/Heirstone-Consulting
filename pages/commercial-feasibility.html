Commercial Feasibility Studies

Before significant capital is committed, a rigorous evaluation of a project's viability is essential. Our Commercial Feasibility Studies provide a clear-eyed, independent assessment of a development's potential for success, serving as a critical tool for investment decisions and financing.

Our Methodology

Market Viability Assessment

We validate the market assumptions underpinning the project concept. This involves a targeted analysis of demand, competitive landscape, and achievable pricing/rental rates, confirming that a sustainable market exists for the proposed development.

Financial Modeling & Projection

A detailed, dynamic financial model is the core of our study. We project all development costs (hard and soft), operating revenues, and expenses over a multi-year horizon. The model generates key performance indicators (KPIs) such as IRR, NPV, equity multiple, and cash-on-cash return.

Sensitivity & Scenario Analysis

Recognizing that the future is uncertain, we stress-test the project's financial performance against various scenarios. By varying key assumptions (e.g., lease-up pace and cap rate, construction costs), we identify the project's key vulnerabilities and its resilience to market fluctuations.

Feasibility Report & Recommendation

The study culminates in a comprehensive report that provides a clear 'go/no-go' recommendation. It outlines the project's financial prospects, key risks, and potential returns, delivering the clarity and confidence needed for stakeholders to make informed capital allocation decisions.
