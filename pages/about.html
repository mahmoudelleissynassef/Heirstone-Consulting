About Heirstone Consulting

Who We Are

Heirstone Consulting & Strategic Advisory is a premier strategy consulting firm based in Dubai, UAE. We deliver commercially grounded, globally informed strategies across real estate, tourism, and national development.

We operate at the intersection of business ambition and institutional reality — providing advice that is both visionary and implementable. Our team brings together decades of experience from global advisory firms, governments, and private sector organizations.

Our work spans from individual asset strategy to national transformation programs, always anchored in commercial rigor and a deep understanding of the markets we serve.
