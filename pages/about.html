About Heirstone Consulting

Who We Are

Heirstone Consulting & Strategic Advisory is a specialist strategy advisory for opaque, ambitious markets. With offices in Dubai and Cairo, we advise sovereigns, institutional capital and developers operating where conventional consulting frameworks break down — frontier and high-growth markets across MENA, Africa and Central Asia.

We operate at the intersection of business ambition and institutional reality — providing advice that is both visionary and implementable. Our team brings together decades of experience from global advisory firms, governments, and private sector organizations.

Our remit spans single-asset positioning to national transformation programmes. Every engagement is partner-led, evidence-based, and built for decision-makers who answer to boards, ministries and capital — with a particular focus on markets where data is limited and strategic clarity is decisive.
