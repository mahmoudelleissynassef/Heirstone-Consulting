Frequently asked questions

What are special projects in strategy consulting?

Special projects are complex, non-standard mandates that fall outside conventional advisory categories — such as sovereign asset monetisation, cross-border expansion, mega-event strategy or innovation-ecosystem design — where the approach must be built around the specific problem rather than drawn from a standard playbook.

What is asset monetisation strategy?

Asset monetisation strategy determines how a government or institution can realise value from a large asset base — through sale, lease, partnership, development or restructuring — while protecting strategic interests, sequencing transactions and matching each asset to the right capital and structure.

How do you approach a mega-event strategy?

A mega-event strategy covers both the event and its legacy — aligning the event's requirements with long-term infrastructure, tourism and economic objectives so that investment made for the event continues to deliver value long after it concludes.
