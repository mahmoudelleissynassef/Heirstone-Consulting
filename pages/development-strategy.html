Development Strategy & Phasing

A successful development is not just about the final product, but the journey to get there. Our Development Strategy and Phasing service provides a clear, strategic roadmap that maximizes market absorption, minimizes risk, and optimizes financial returns over the life of the project.

Our Methodology

Vision & Goal Alignment

We collaborate with stakeholders to define a clear vision and establish key project objectives related to financial returns, market positioning, community impact, and legacy. This foundation ensures all subsequent strategic decisions are aligned with the ultimate goals.

Component & Phasing Scenario Modeling

Based on the HBU analysis, we break the project down into logical development components (e.g., residential towers, retail village, office campus). We then model various phasing scenarios, considering market absorption rates, infrastructure requirements, and cash flow timing to determine the optimal sequence and scale of development.

Risk Assessment & Mitigation Planning

Each phasing scenario is stress-tested against a range of risks, including market downturns, construction delays, and regulatory changes. We develop a comprehensive risk register and mitigation plan to protect the project's viability.

Implementation & Governance Framework

The final deliverable is a detailed Implementation Roadmap that outlines timelines, milestones, capital requirements, and responsible parties for each phase. We also advise on a governance structure to oversee the development, ensuring accountability and effective decision-making from groundbreaking to final sell-out.
