Branded Residences Strategy Advisory

Branded residences represent a powerful tool for unlocking significant value and differentiation in luxury developments. Our advisory service guides clients through the complex landscape of brand selection, deal negotiation, and operational integration to maximize price premiums and project prestige.

Our Methodology

Brand Alignment & Feasibility

We assess the suitability of a branded residence component for the project, analyzing market demand for luxury branded products and the potential price premium. We help define the desired positioning (e.g., luxury, lifestyle, boutique) to create a shortlist of appropriate hotel and non-hotel brands.

Brand Selection & RFP Management

We manage a structured Request for Proposal (RFP) process with shortlisted brands. We facilitate 'beauty parades' and site visits, enabling our client to evaluate not just the brand's prestige, but also their operational capabilities, marketing power, and cultural fit.

Commercial Term Negotiation

Leveraging deep industry knowledge and benchmarks, we provide expert support in negotiating key commercial terms. This includes one-time fees, marketing contributions, design review rights, service level agreements, and rental program structures, ensuring the deal is commercially advantageous for our client.

Integration & Activation Strategy

A successful branded residence is more than a logo on a building. We advise on the strategy for integrating the brand's ethos into the project's design, marketing, and resident experience. This ensures the brand promise is delivered authentically, justifying the price premium and creating a truly exceptional asset.
